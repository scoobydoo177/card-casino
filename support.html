❓Frequently Asked Questions

I ran out of chips. How do I get more?

When your chips are low, a button appears on the home screen to refill back to 2,000. You can also watch a short rewarded video ad inside either game to earn 500 bonus chips. In Poker, a Reset Chips button appears when you reach 0.

How do I change the difficulty?

Tap Easy, Medium, or Hard on the home screen before starting a game. You can also tap the difficulty badge in the top-right corner of the Blackjack screen to cycle through difficulties between hands.

What does Split mean in Blackjack?

If your first two cards have the same rank (e.g. two 8s), a Split button appears. Tapping it creates two separate hands — each gets a new card dealt — and deducts a second bet equal to your original. You play each hand in sequence, then the dealer plays once against both.

What does Double Down mean?

Double Down is only available on your first two cards. It doubles your current bet and deals exactly one more card, then automatically stands. It's a high-risk, high-reward move best used when you have a total of 10 or 11.

How does Texas Hold'em work?

You play against three AI opponents (Marcus, Elena, and Jake). Each player gets two hole cards, then five community cards are revealed across Pre-flop, Flop, Turn, and River betting rounds. The best 5-card hand from your 2 hole cards and the 5 community cards wins the pot.

Do the AI opponents cheat?

No. AI opponents only see their own hole cards, just like a real game. On Hard difficulty they play closer to optimal strategy, but they have no knowledge of your cards.

Why does the dealer in Blackjack hit on 17 in Hard mode?

The difficulty affects the dealer's stand threshold — the total at which they stop drawing cards. Easy: stand at 16. Medium: stand at 17 (standard casino rules). Hard: stand at 18, meaning the dealer draws an extra card more often.

Will my chips carry over if I delete and reinstall the app?

No. Chip balances are stored locally on your device and will be reset to 2,000 if you uninstall the app. There is no cloud save or account system.

Is there real money involved?

No. Card Casino uses virtual chips with no monetary value. There are no real-money purchases, no betting of real funds, and no way to withdraw or exchange chips. It is a simulation for entertainment only.

The app crashed or a card got stuck. What should I do?

Force-close the app and reopen it. Your chip balance is saved automatically between sessions. If the issue keeps happening, please contact us with your device model and iOS version so we can investigate.

How do I opt out of personalized ads?

Go to iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Card Casino and toggle off "Allow Tracking." You'll still see ads, but they won't be personalized to your interests.
