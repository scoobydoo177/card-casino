App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14.5 and later, Apple requires apps to ask for permission before accessing your device's Advertising ID (IDFA) for cross-app tracking. If the App presents this prompt, you can choose to allow or deny tracking. Denying will not affect your ability to play the game — you will still see ads, but they will be less personalized.

You can also change your tracking preference at any time in:

iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Card Casino

Advertising ID (IDFA) — How to Limit or Reset

Apple provides built-in controls to limit ad tracking. To reset or limit your Advertising ID:

iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads (toggle off)

Resetting your Advertising ID does not delete data already associated with the previous ID.
